Questions
Frequently Asked
How much does custom furniture cost?
Material costs for a typical base cabinet are $150–$300 depending on plywood grade and hardware selection. Maker labor is quoted separately and varies by region. The configurator shows you exact material costs before you submit.
Do I need to know anything about woodworking?
No. The configurator handles all the engineering — joinery, clearances, structural calculations. You just pick dimensions and style. The plans tell the maker exactly what to cut and how to assemble.
How long does it take?
Configuration takes 5–10 minutes online. Build time depends on complexity: a simple bookcase might be 1–2 days, a full kitchen suite could be 2–4 weeks. Your maker provides the timeline.
What furniture types are available?
17 templates: kitchen base, kitchen wall, bathroom vanity, bookcase, pantry tower, media console, wardrobe, filing cabinet, nightstand, mudroom locker, desk pedestal, shoe cabinet, wine cabinet, garage cabinet, linen cabinet, kitchen island, and floating vanity.
Can I add decorative elements?
Yes. Our LamiForm module creates turned legs, bun feet, corbels, and other 3D parts from flat CNC-cut slices. 18 profile presets with parametric control. These attach to any cabinet chassis.
Where is this available?
The configurator is available worldwide. Maker matching is currently being built — sign up to be notified when it launches in your area.
