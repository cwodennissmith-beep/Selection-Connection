17 Furniture Types
Kitchen base, wall cabinet, vanity, bookcase, wardrobe, desk, island, pantry, and more. One-click preset loading with full parametric control.

49 Parametric Sliders
Every dimension, clearance, overlay, and thickness is adjustable. Real-time validation warns when values conflict or exceed structural limits.

Hardware Intelligence
Select Blum, Hettich, Grass, or other brands. Boring patterns and clearances auto-adjust. Full hardware schedule with quantities and cost.

Instant Cut Lists
Every part computed live: sides, bottom, back, shelves, doors, drawer boxes, stretchers. Sheet goods calculator with waste factor.

Production Exports
PDF plans (free). CNC-ready DXF with 4 layers: CUT, DRILL, ENGRAVE, REFERENCE. CSV BOM with hardware and cost summary.
